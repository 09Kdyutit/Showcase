# Showcase Waitlist Copy Reference

## Core tagline
Turn your experience into evidence.

## Positioning statement
Showcase turns resumes, projects, and work history into proof-of-work portfolios — then audits them with ProofScore to show exactly what is weak, what evidence is missing, and what to fix before applying.

## Who this is for
- Students building their first portfolio
- New grads entering a competitive market
- Early career professionals with no portfolio
- Career switchers who need to reframe their experience
- Designers, developers, PMs, marketers, researchers
- Freelancers who need a professional proof-of-work site
- Anyone who has been rejected and does not know why

## The transformation
Before: Messy resume, no portfolio, no idea what recruiters actually want
After: Polished proof-of-work portfolio + ProofScore audit + specific next fixes

## What ProofScore audits (11 dimensions)
1. First Impression Clarity
2. Target Role Alignment
3. Proof Strength (the most important)
4. Project Depth
5. Resume Quality
6. Case Study Quality
7. Credibility Signals
8. Visual Polish
9. Contact Readiness
10. Keyword Relevance
11. Hiring Risk Gaps

## Safe copy to use
- "Get early access"
- "Join the private beta"
- "Help shape Showcase"
- "Build a stronger proof-of-work portfolio"
- "Find the evidence gaps in your resume or portfolio"
- "We never invent experience"
- "You control what gets published"
- "Resume files stay private by default"
- "Turn your experience into evidence"
- "A portfolio that proves what you can do"
- "Know exactly what to fix before you apply"
- "Be honest with yourself about your portfolio"

## Banned copy — never use
- guaranteed hired / guarantee a job / guaranteed interviews
- beat ATS / hack ATS / trick the algorithm
- hack recruiters / fool recruiters
- fake experience / inflate metrics
- AI gets you hired / Showcase gets you hired
- instant job / instant interviews
- almost guaranteed
- 100% success rate
- X users / Y portfolios built (until real and verified)
- Any specific conversion percentage claims
- Fake testimonials or logos

## Trust statements

- "We never invent experience, employers, projects, or certifications."
- "You control what gets published — nothing goes live without your review."
- "Resume files stay private by default."
- "You can request deletion of your account and data at any time."
- "No hiring guarantees, no fake metrics, no dark patterns."
- "Feedback is confidential. We never share raw responses publicly without permission."

## FAQ answers (canonical)
**Is the beta free?**
Yes. Beta access is completely free.

**When will I get access?**
We invite users in small batches. We will email you when your spot is ready.

**Do I need a resume?**
A resume helps but is not required. You can start with project descriptions, links, and work history.

**Will Showcase write fake experience?**
Never. Showcase only works with what you provide.

**Will this guarantee a job or interview?**
No. A stronger portfolio improves your presentation — outcomes depend on many other factors.

**How is my data used?**
Email is used for beta invites and product updates only. We never sell it. Unsubscribe anytime.

**Can I give feedback?**
That is exactly why we are doing a beta.

**Can I unsubscribe?**
Yes. Every email has an unsubscribe link.
