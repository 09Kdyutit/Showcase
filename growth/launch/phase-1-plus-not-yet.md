## When Gate 1 is met, this is what comes next (in order)

### 1. Open waitlist to general public
- Remove invite-only gate
- Let anyone sign up on `/waitlist` and auto-get access
- Set up a simple welcome email sequence (email 1 at signup, email 2 at day 3 if no activation)

### 2. Product Hunt launch
- Build a hunter relationship (1 person who will hunt it)
- Prepare 3 quality screenshots from real published portfolios
- Write the tagline and description — no invented results, only real beta outcomes
- Prepare 5 maker comments from real beta users (with `testimonial_permission = true`)
- Target a Tuesday or Wednesday launch
- Do not launch until at least 3 real portfolios exist that you'd be proud to screenshot

### 3. Content: "I made a portfolio in 15 minutes" post
- Wait until a beta user does this naturally
- Ask for permission to share their story (with `testimonial_permission`)
- Post on LinkedIn and Twitter with their portfolio as the screenshot
- No invented results — only real outcomes

### 4. SEO: Landing pages for specific roles
- "Portfolio for UX designers"
- "Portfolio for software engineers"
- "Portfolio for data scientists"
- "Portfolio for career switchers"
- Each page: 600–800 words, real examples, no fake testimonials
- Do not build until 5+ real portfolios per persona exist as examples

### 5. Free tool launch (ProofScore Preview)
- See `growth/free-tool/spec.md`
- Build and launch after Gate 1

### 6. Referral program
- The waitlist already generates referral codes
- Activate a referral incentive only after you can fulfill it (e.g., 1 month Pro free for each successful referral who publishes)
- Do not promise incentives you haven't built yet

### 7. Paid ads
- Only after Gate 2 (`phase-gate.md`)
- Start with $10/day on LinkedIn targeting "open to work" + specific roles
- Do not run paid ads until Pro conversion rate ≥ 5%

## What we are NOT doing (ever)

- Fake social proof (invented testimonials, made-up user counts)
- Claims about job outcomes we can't prove ("get hired 3x faster")
- Guaranteed results of any kind
- Product Hunt launch without real user portfolios to show
- Paying for reviews or testimonials
