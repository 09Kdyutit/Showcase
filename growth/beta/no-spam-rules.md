# No-Spam Rules

These rules are non-negotiable. Violating them kills beta trust and our sender reputation.

---

## Hard rules — never do these

1. **No cold mass DMs.** Every DM is personal and references something specific about the recipient.
2. **No more than 2 follow-ups total** per person. If someone hasn't responded after 2 messages, stop.
3. **No DMs to people who didn't ask.** Only reach out to people who: joined the waitlist, replied to a post, commented on something relevant, or are a personal connection.
4. **No fake urgency.** Never say "only 5 spots left" unless it's literally true and you can prove it.
5. **No fake social proof.** Never invent user numbers, results, or testimonials.
6. **No recruiting from competitor products.** Don't scrape LinkedIn or other platforms to find users.
7. **No posting the same message in multiple channels simultaneously.** Customize per channel.
8. **No Reddit self-promotion in subreddits that forbid it.** Always check sidebar rules first.
9. **No inviting people who already told you no.** Track declines and honor them.
10. **No auto-DM tools.** Every message must be typed by a human.

---

## Soft rules — strong defaults

- Wait at least 48 hours between a waitlist signup and an invite
- Personalize every invite with the person's name, target role, or biggest challenge from their form
- Always give an easy out: "no obligation, just wanted to share"
- Don't send invites on Fridays or weekends (lower open rates, feels spammy)
- Don't discuss pricing or PRO features in outreach — focus on solving the problem

---

## Acceptable outreach channels

| Channel | OK to use | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Email | Yes | Only to people who opted in via waitlist |
| LinkedIn | Yes | Only personal connections or 1st-degree with personalized note |
| Twitter/X DM | Yes | Only people who engaged with your posts or are in your network |
| Reddit | Limited | Only in threads where it's clearly relevant and rules allow |
| Discord / Slack | Limited | Only in channels explicitly for beta/product feedback |
| Cold phone | No | Never |
| Instagram DM | No | Wrong audience |

---

## When someone says no

Log it in `beta-user-tracker.csv` with status `declined`.  
Do not contact them again for any reason.  
If they say "maybe later," wait 30+ days before a single gentle re-engagement.
