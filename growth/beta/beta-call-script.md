# Beta Call Script (30 minutes)

Use this for screen-share sessions with beta users.  
Goal: observe them completing the core loop. Do not teach. Do not help.

---

## Before the call (5 min prep)

- Review their waitlist form: `biggest_challenge`, `target_role`, `experience_level`
- Check their `usage_events` in Supabase — what have they done so far?
- If they've published: look at their portfolio at `/p/[slug]`
- Open `user-observation-sheet.md` and have it ready to fill in real time
- Set up screen recording if they consented (ask explicitly at the start)

---

## Opening (2 min)

> "Thanks for taking the time. I'll keep this to 30 minutes.
>
> Here's what I'd love to do: I want to watch you use Showcase the way you would if I weren't here. I'm not going to teach you anything or help you — I just want to see where it clicks and where it doesn't. If you get stuck, that's actually the most valuable thing you can show me.
>
> Does that make sense? Any questions before we start?
>
> And just to check — are you okay with me recording this session for my own notes?"

---

## Core loop observation (20 min, they drive)

**Let them drive completely. Your only job is to observe and note.**

Watch for:
- Where do they pause or hesitate?
- Where do they re-read something?
- Where do they click something that doesn't do what they expected?
- What do they say out loud (unprompted)?
- What do they skip or skim?
- Do they notice the ProofScore section?
- Do they make edits to the generated portfolio?
- Do they hit publish? If not — why do they stop?

**Interventions (only if completely blocked for more than 3 minutes):**
> "What would you expect to happen if you [do X]?"  
> "What are you thinking right now?"  
> "What would you do next if this were your real portfolio session?"

Do not say "click here," "try this," or fix things for them.

---

## Debrief questions (8 min)

After they've completed as much of the loop as they can, ask these — pick 4–5 based on what you observed:

**On the output:**
1. "When you look at what the AI generated — does that feel like you? Does it tell your story accurately?"
2. "If you showed this to someone hiring for [target role], what would you be worried they'd think?"
3. "What would need to change before you'd be comfortable sharing this link publicly?"

**On the experience:**
4. "Was there a moment where you weren't sure what to do next? Walk me through that."
5. "What surprised you — either positively or negatively?"
6. "If you were describing this to a friend who was also job hunting, what would you say?"

**On the problem:**
7. "Before Showcase, how were you handling the portfolio problem?"
8. "Is this actually better than what you were doing before? By how much?"

**On intent:**
9. "Are you planning to share this portfolio in your job search?"
10. "What would make you feel confident enough to send it to a recruiter?"

---

## Closing (1 min)

> "This has been incredibly helpful. Last thing — is there anything you wanted to say that the questions didn't give you room for?"
>
> "I'll follow up by email with the feedback form link. Would you be open to a quick follow-up call in 2 weeks once you've had a chance to actually use it in your search?"

---

## Immediately after the call (do within 1 hour)

1. Fill out `user-observation-sheet.md` while fresh
2. Add bugs to `top-bugs-template.md`
3. Add feature requests to `top-feature-requests-template.md`
4. Note key quotes verbatim (not paraphrased)
5. Update `beta-user-tracker.csv` — mark `call_completed`
6. Send follow-up email within 24 hours with feedback form link
