# Beta Interview Question Bank

Use these in post-call debriefs or async follow-up emails.  
Pick 4–6 per person — don't ask all of them. Prioritize based on where they got stuck.

---

## About their job search situation

1. Where are you in your job search right now?
2. What does your current application process look like end-to-end?
3. How are you currently sharing your work with employers?
4. What's the biggest thing holding you back right now in the search?
5. Have you gotten feedback from recruiters or interviewers about your materials?

---

## About the portfolio problem

6. Did you have a portfolio before Showcase? What did it look like?
7. Why didn't you have a portfolio / why wasn't yours working?
8. What would an ideal portfolio do for you in your search?
9. When a recruiter or hiring manager looks at your portfolio, what do you want them to feel?
10. What would you need to see in a portfolio tool to trust it with your first impression?

---

## About the Showcase experience

11. Walk me through what happened from the moment you landed on the site to now.
12. Where did you feel confused, hesitant, or unsure?
13. What moment, if any, did it click for you?
14. What was your reaction when you saw the AI-generated output? Honest first impression?
15. Did the generated portfolio feel like you? Did it accurately represent your work?
16. What would you change about the portfolio the AI generated?
17. Was there a point where you considered stopping? What made you consider it?

---

## About the output quality

18. If you sent this portfolio link to a recruiter tomorrow — what would you be worried they'd think?
19. Is there anything about the portfolio you're proud to show? What specifically?
20. On a scale of 1–10: how accurate is the portfolio compared to how you'd describe yourself in an interview?
21. What would need to change before you'd share this with a recruiter or hiring manager?

---

## About the publish decision

22. Did you publish? If not, what was missing?
23. If you published: are you actually using the link in applications?
24. Have you shown it to anyone? What did they say?
25. What would make you feel confident enough to put this link in your email signature?

---

## About product direction

26. What's the one thing that, if we fixed it, would make you recommend Showcase to every friend in a job search?
27. What's missing that you expected to be there?
28. Is there anything Showcase does that you didn't expect? (positive or negative)
29. Would you pay for this? If yes, how much and what would you expect at that price?
30. Who else do you know who needs this right now? Would you send them a link?

---

## Closing

31. What do you wish you'd known before starting?
32. Anything I didn't ask that you think I should know?

---

## Usage guidelines

- Never lead the witness: don't say "was it confusing when X happened?" — say "walk me through what happened when X"
- Silence is data: if they pause, let them. Don't fill the gap.
- Follow up on anything surprising: if they say something unexpected, dig with "tell me more about that"
- Write down verbatim quotes — paraphrasing loses nuance
