## Core loop observations

### Resume upload / paste
- Did they upload or paste?
- How long did it take to find where to paste?
- Any hesitation or confusion?
- Did they read the instructions or skip them?

### Portfolio generation
- Did they set target role before generating?
- How long did they wait during generation?
- What was their first reaction when they saw the output?
- Did they scroll through the whole thing or skim?

### Editing
- Did they make any edits?
- What did they change first?
- What did they leave as-is even if it was wrong?
- Was the editor interface intuitive?

### Publishing
- Did they publish?
- If yes: how long from first login to published?
- If no: where did they stop and why?
- Did they notice the public URL / `/p/slug` format?
