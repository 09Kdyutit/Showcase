# Direct DM Scripts

25 variants for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Discord, and Slack.  
Always read `no-spam-rules.md` before using these. Every message must be personal.

---

## Core principle

Reference something real: their tweet, their LinkedIn post, their role, their problem. Never send a generic message.

---

## Twitter/X DMs (10 variants)

### T1 — They tweeted about job hunting
> Hey [name] — saw your tweet about the job search. I'm building something that might help: turns your resume into a proper portfolio that shows impact, not just titles. Built it because I had the same problem. Would love for you to try it early if you're open to it.

### T2 — They tweeted about resume frustration  
> Your tweet about [resume thing] resonated. I'm building Showcase — it takes your resume and turns it into a portfolio page that actually shows what you did. Early beta, no polish yet, but it solves that exact problem. Want early access?

### T3 — They tweeted about a portfolio they liked
> That portfolio you shared is really good. I'm building something that generates portfolios like that from a resume — takes 15 minutes. Would love to know if it measures up. Want to try it?

### T4 — They're a student who just graduated
> Congrats on graduating! I'm building a tool for new grads who need a portfolio that shows their projects' impact, not just the projects themselves. Beta's open to a small group. Would you be up for trying it?

### T5 — They're a designer looking for work
> Saw you're open to new opportunities. I'm building Showcase — it turns your resume/case studies into a proof-led portfolio page. Would love a designer's honest opinion on it. Free access while we're in beta.

### T6 — They retweeted something about job searching
> Noticed you're navigating the job hunt — I'm building a tool specifically for that transition. Takes your resume, generates a portfolio that leads with your impact. 15 min to a published page. Want early access? No cost, just honest feedback in return.

### T7 — They're a developer
> Hey [name] — building a tool that turns a resume into a portfolio with proof of impact. Figured a developer would have good taste in what's actually useful vs what's noise. Beta's small, no charge. Interested?

### T8 — They posted about career change
> Saw you're making a career switch — that's exactly who I built Showcase for. Your old resume won't tell the story you need. I'm in early beta and looking for people to try it before launch. Would love your take.

### T9 — They're a freelancer
> Hey [name] — your work looks great. I'm building something to help freelancers attract better clients by turning their resume/work history into a proof-led portfolio. Beta's free, would love to know if it fits your workflow.

### T10 — They engaged with something you posted
> Thanks for the reply on that — since you're thinking about portfolios, I'd love for you to try Showcase. It's in early beta. Takes your resume and generates a portfolio page in about 15 minutes. Honest feedback welcome.

## LinkedIn DMs (8 variants)

*LinkedIn notes on connection requests are ≤300 characters. Full DMs can be longer.*

### L1 — Connection note (≤300 chars)
> Hi [name], saw your background in [field] — I'm building Showcase, a tool that turns resumes into proof-led portfolios. Early beta, would love your honest feedback. Worth a 15-minute try?

### L2 — Connection note (≤300 chars)
> Hi [name] — we're both in [shared connection/field]. I'm in early beta with a portfolio tool built for [their role]. Turns resume into portfolio in 15 min. Interested in trying it?

### L3 — After connecting, first DM
> Thanks for connecting, [name]. I wanted to reach out because I saw you're [target role / open to work / recently graduated]. I'm building Showcase — a tool that generates a portfolio from your resume that leads with impact and proof, not job titles. We're in private beta with a small group. Would you be open to trying it and sharing honest feedback? No cost involved.

### L4 — Active job seeker
> Hi [name], I noticed you're open to new opportunities. I'm building Showcase — early beta, generates a proof-led portfolio page from your resume in about 15 minutes. Designed specifically for people in active job searches. Would you be interested in trying it? I'm looking for honest feedback from people who actually need it right now.

### L5 — Recent grad
> Hi [name], congratulations on [recent graduation / new role / certification]. I'm building a portfolio tool for people who want to show the impact of their work, not just a list of jobs. Early beta — free access, just need honest feedback. Interested?

### L6 — Designer
> Hi [name], your portfolio work is great. I'm building something that generates a proof-led portfolio from a resume — specifically built for designers who have strong case studies but want a better presentation layer. Would love a designer's perspective. Early beta, no charge.

### L7 — Career switcher
> Hi [name], I saw you made the transition from [old field] to [new field]. That's a hard story to tell on a resume. I'm building Showcase — it takes your resume and generates a portfolio that reframes your experience for the role you want. Early beta. Would love to know if it actually solves the problem.

### L8 — Developer
> Hi [name], I'm building a portfolio tool for developers and would love an engineer's perspective. Generates a portfolio from a resume in about 15 minutes. Early beta, free access. Would you be up for trying it and being honest about what doesn't work?

## Discord / Slack DMs (7 variants)

*Use only in communities where you're already an active member. Never DM strangers in Discord cold.*

### D1 — Career/job-hunting channel
> Hey, I've been lurking here and building something that might be useful for people in the job hunt. It's called Showcase — takes your resume and generates a portfolio page that shows impact, not just job titles. I'm in early beta with a small group and looking for honest feedback. Would anyone here be interested in trying it? Happy to give free access in exchange for 5 minutes of honest thoughts.

### D2 — Designer community
> Quick question for anyone here who's looked for work recently — what did your portfolio situation look like? I'm building something that automates the portfolio step, and I'd love to try it with a few designers who have opinions. Early beta, no cost.

### D3 — Developer community
> Not sure if this fits here, but I'm building a tool that generates a portfolio page from a resume. Specifically for devs who have shipped things but haven't had time to make a proper portfolio site. Early beta. Would anyone be willing to try it and tell me what's broken?

### D4 — Reply to someone asking about portfolios
> Hey [name] — saw your question about portfolios. I'm building something that might help: it takes your resume and generates a portfolio with your proof of impact automatically. Early beta, no charge. Would you be interested in trying it?

### D5 — Reply to someone asking for resume feedback
> [name] — before I give feedback on the resume: have you considered a portfolio instead? The stuff you've shipped is hard to see in a resume. I'm building a tool that generates one from a resume in 15 minutes. Happy to give you access and see if the output is better than the CV.

### D6 — New grads channel
> For anyone who just graduated or is in the early job search: I'm building a portfolio tool specifically for people who don't have a big work history yet but do have projects and impact to show. Early beta, free. DM me if you're interested in trying it.

### D7 — Startup / product community
> Working on something in the portfolio space — Showcase generates a proof-led portfolio from a resume using AI. Early beta. If anyone here is a designer, developer, or career switcher who needs a portfolio and wants early access, I'd love to try it with you. Honest feedback only.
