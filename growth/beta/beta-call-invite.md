Send to: users who activated but haven't published after 5+ days, OR users who published and you want qualitative depth from.  
Goal: 30-minute screen share / walkthrough call.

---

**Subject:** 30 minutes? I'd love to watch you use Showcase — [NAME]

---

Hi [NAME],

I have a specific ask: would you be willing to share your screen for 30 minutes while you use Showcase?

I'm not looking to teach you anything. I just want to watch where you get stuck, what you skip, and what you wish worked differently. That kind of observation is 10x more valuable than any survey.

You'd be helping shape the product for everyone who comes after you.

Totally optional. If you're up for it, I'll send a Zoom link.

—Vinay

---

## Who to prioritize for calls

1. Users who signed up but haven't generated a portfolio after 3+ days
2. Users who generated but haven't published after 3+ days
3. Users who published something that looks incomplete
4. Users who gave a low rating (< 6) in beta feedback
5. Users who mentioned specific bugs or frustrations in feedback

## Call goals (pick 1–2 per call)

- Watch them complete the core loop from scratch
- Watch them edit a generated portfolio
- Understand what stopped them from publishing
- Get raw verbal reactions to portfolio quality

## After the call

- Fill out `user-observation-sheet.md` within 1 hour
- Note key quotes and blockers in tracker
- Add any bugs to `top-bugs-template.md`
- Add feature requests to `top-feature-requests-template.md`
