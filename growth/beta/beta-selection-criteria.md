# Beta User Selection Criteria

We are selecting 20–50 people from the waitlist. Quality over speed.

---

## Disqualifying criteria (auto-reject)

- No response to "biggest challenge" field (blank = low motivation)
- Email domain looks fake or disposable
- Used referral spam (obvious pattern in referral_code sequence)
- Experience level: not actively in a job search or career transition
- Submitted via automated tool (honeypot field filled)

---

## Scoring rubric (0–10, select anyone ≥ 6)

| Signal | Max points | How to score |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| **Urgency** — actively job searching or need a portfolio now | 3 | 3 = "need job ASAP", 2 = "looking in next 3 months", 1 = "someday", 0 = unclear |
| **Articulation** — `biggest_challenge` field is specific and personal | 3 | 3 = named a real problem clearly, 2 = vague but genuine, 1 = generic, 0 = blank |
| **Portfolio gap** — no existing portfolio, or has a bad one | 2 | 2 = no portfolio, 1 = bad portfolio listed, 0 = has a good portfolio already |
| **Target segment** — fits a primary persona | 2 | 2 = student/new grad or designer/dev or switcher, 1 = adjacent, 0 = doesn't fit |

**Score ≥ 7:** Invite in wave 1 (first 20)  
**Score 6:** Invite in wave 2 (next 20)  
**Score < 6:** Keep on waitlist, don't invite yet

---

## Priority segments

### Tier 1 — Highest conversion likelihood
- Final-year CS/design students with no portfolio
- New grads (< 2 years experience) applying for first job
- Designers / frontend devs with case studies but no portfolio site

### Tier 2 — High pain, slower to convert
- Career switchers with 5–10 years experience, entering tech
- Freelancers wanting to attract better clients

### Tier 3 — Interesting but deprioritize
- Mid/senior engineers (may not see value in portfolio)
- Anyone with a strong existing portfolio

---

## Cohort sizes

| Wave | Size | When to send | Goal |
|------|------|-------------|------|
| Wave 1 | 10 | Day 1 | Find the fastest path to first publish |
| Wave 2 | 15 | Day 7 (if wave 1 has ≥3 published) | Expand, get more signal |
| Wave 3 | 25 | Day 14 (if wave 2 conversion ≥ 40%) | Scale proven patterns |

Do not send wave 2 until wave 1 conversion rate (published/invited) ≥ 30%.

---

## Manual review checklist

For each candidate:
- [ ] Read their `biggest_challenge` answer carefully
- [ ] Check their `target_role` — is it realistic?
- [ ] Check `experience_level` — are they currently in motion?
- [ ] Score 0–10 using rubric above
- [ ] Assign to a segment: student / designer-dev / switcher / freelancer
- [ ] Note any personalization hook for invite email
